About Us

Smart SUPPLY is a web-based internal forecasting system built in collaboration with Sports Direct to tackle the complex challenges of demand planning and inventory control across sports retail operations.

Developed by Business Analytics students as a capstone project, the system merges academic insight with real business needs to help stakeholders make smart, data-driven decisions.

Our Vision

To deliver a high-impact, user-friendly system that demonstrates the power of business analytics in the retail sector—and to contribute meaningful value to Sports Direct as part of our academic journey.

Our Mission

To apply our academic knowledge and analytical skills to create a practical, data-driven solution that helps Sports Direct optimize demand forecasting accuracy and thus enhance decision-making.

What We Do

Our system is designed to support retail operations by:

  • Forecasting Product Demand: Leveraging deep learning and sales data to anticipate future trends and customer needs.
  • Streamlining Inventory Management: Minimizing stockouts and overstock situations through smarter stock planning.
  • Providing Actionable Insights: Interactive dashboards to help managers make informed, real-time decisions.

Why This Matters

Retailers like Sports Direct face constant challenges in balancing supply with demand. Our project provides a scalable solution that supports data-driven planning—boosting efficiency, reducing waste, and ultimately improving the customer experience.
